Common Sense

Common sense is not a genetically inherited trait. Its a character that everyone can develop. However, it takes alot of hardwork and commitment to improve them. Personality is malleable and can be altered for better; based on the preference of the person.


Socially, we all are in high demand for common sense otherwise the pace of human development would be slowed down.

Intelligence..

Similarly, intelligence is an acquired skill. Science has done its job in proving how someone can increase the neurological connections and agreed on the golden rule of 'Practice makes perfect'

In conclusion

Self-awareness is the key to identifying the traits that we should improve, committing to changing them is the second.
